Calibration controls

Calibration state controls

Control-TabToggle calibration mode. In calibration mode, the normal distribution of events is interrupted. Instead of being sent to the application, events are routed instead to the application configuration. This allows view frusta and display modes to be tuned interactively.
Control-SpaceCycle forward through the list of configurable tiles.
Control-BackspaceCycle backward through the list of configurable tiles.
Control-ReturnCycle through the list of display modes.

Varrier calibration controls

While in calibration mode, with a Varrier display mode selected...

LeftShift decrease 0.00005
RightShift increase 0.00005
UpOptical thickness decrease 0.0001
DownOptical thickness increase 0.0001
Shift-LeftAngle decrease 0.01
Shift-RightAngle increase by 0.01
Shift-UpPitch increase 0.01
Shift-DownPitch decrease 0.01
